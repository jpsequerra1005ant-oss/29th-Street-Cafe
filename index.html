Our Story

Located in Marikina City, our business is dedicated to serving students from elementary to college, especially with our convenient location near schools. We are committed to providing quality products and excellent customer service, creating a welcoming space where students can easily access what they need.

Our mission is to grow as a community-centered business that builds trust and customer loyalty through consistent service and reliable offerings. Despite challenges such as strong competition from nearby coffee shops, we remain focused on improving our services and strengthening our presence in the community.
